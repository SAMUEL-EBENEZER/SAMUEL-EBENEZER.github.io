About us

FocusLearning is an online training platform with highly qualified teachers from the top universities. We offer online based live one-on-one and group classes for students who want to learn math and computer science concepts. Also our classes can be conducted through online video calling with the shared white board so that the instructor and the learner can communicate effectively with each other at their convenient place.

We offer classes to all grade students from different backgrounds like CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, not only restricted to India but also students from USA, UK and other curricula and also college going students from Mathematics and Computer Science background. We mentor people who want to prepare themselves for various competitive exams, Olympiads, screening test for IT careers, coding challenges and other skill based learning curriculum and also for individuals who want to take part in various national and international level entrance exams. In addition to teaching concepts, we also personally guide the candidate personally in the field of academic and co-curricular spheres.
